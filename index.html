Fruity Marketing 02.01.14

Joe Cross gives away recipies and copies of his film " Fat Sick & Nearly Dead" for FREE.
Bananas?
There is method in his madness.

Step 1
Do a branded challenge, in his case "60 days consuming only juice"
Step 2
Make a movie documenting what you learn and submit to film festivals
Step 3
Write a recipie book. Joe is now an authority on Juicing.
Step 4
Put toghether a website and install affiliate links partnering with blender manifactures
Step 5
Get a timely spread in the January edition The Sunday Times Style magazine (- distribution 10 million) talking about a "Juice Reboot" and advertising a link to the free copy of the film ( exclusively for readers in exchange for an email address)
Step 6
Sit back, sip juice , email your expanded distribution list about your new movie/ book / gym.
